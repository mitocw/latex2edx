All single qubit unitary operations can be expressed, up to a global phase, as a rotation about an axis in Bloch sphere space. If the axis is [mathjaxinline]\hat n=[n_ x,n_ y,n_ z][/mathjaxinline] (a unit vector) and the rotation angle is [mathjaxinline]\theta[/mathjaxinline], then the rotation may be written in terms of the Pauli matrices as
